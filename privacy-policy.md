# Privacy Policy for Mumin - Islamic Companion App

**Effective Date:** December 19, 2025

Mumin ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Mumin app.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

- **Location Information:** To provide accurate prayer times, Qibla direction, and Ramadan schedules.
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version, and language preferences.
- **Usage Data:** How you use the app to improve features and performance.
- **Optional Information:** Audio recordings for Quran practice, if you choose to use this feature.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

- Provide accurate prayer times, Ramadan calendar, and Qibla direction.
- Enhance app features and improve user experience.
- Send notifications for prayer times and Ramadan alerts (if enabled).

We **do not sell or share your personal information** with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Mumin may use third-party services for:

- Maps and mosque location services
- Analytics and crash reporting

These services may collect limited data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. 

## Children's Privacy

Mumin is suitable for all ages. We **do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13**. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us, and we will remove it.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on GitHub with the revised effective date.
